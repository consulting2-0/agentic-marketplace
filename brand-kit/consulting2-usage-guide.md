## 🖥️ POWERPOINT / KEYNOTE

1. Open `consulting2-logo-primary.svg` in Inkscape (free) or Adobe Illustrator
2. Export as **PNG at 300dpi** (1200×240px minimum)
3. Save as PNG with **transparent background**
4. In PowerPoint: Insert → Pictures → your PNG
5. Set slide background color to `#040D20` (Space)
6. Add slide accent bar: thin rectangle, color `#0057FF`

**Slide font setup:**
- Headlines: Install **Syne** free from fonts.google.com → search "Syne"
- Body: **Inter** from fonts.google.com
- Code blocks: **JetBrains Mono** from fonts.google.com

**PowerPoint theme colors to set:**
- Dark 1: `#040D20` | Dark 2: `#071535`
- Accent 1: `#0057FF` | Accent 2: `#00E599`
- Accent 3: `#FF8C00` | Accent 4: `#7B4FFF`

## 📺 YOUTUBE

### Channel Art (2560×1440px)
- Background: `#040D20`
- Safe zone (all devices): center 1546×423px
- Logo: centered, ~400px wide
- Tagline: "The Best Open Source Agentic Marketplace for Enterprise Intelligence"
- Tools to create: **Canva** (free) → YouTube Channel Art template → customize colors

### Thumbnail Template (1280×720px)
- Left half: dark bg `#040D20` with logo + topic
- Right half: screenshot or graphic
- Title text: Syne Bold, 60–80pt, white or `#00E599`
- Badge/label: small pill in `#0057FF`
- Always add a thin `#00E599` bottom border strip (10px)

### Intro/Outro Cards
- Duration: 5 seconds
- Background: dark grid pattern (see brand kit)
- Animate the hexagon logo rotating in
- End card: logo + "Subscribe" CTA in `#0057FF` button

## 📱 INSTAGRAM / REELS

### Profile Picture
Use `consulting2-icon-mark.svg` → export as 800×800px PNG

### Feed Posts (1080×1080px square)
**Template structure:**
- Background: `#040D20` with subtle dot grid
- Left accent bar: 8px, color `#0057FF`
- Logo: top-left, ~180px wide
- Main text: Syne 800, 64–80pt, white
- Accent word: in `#00E599` or `#0057FF`
- Bottom: thin `#00E599` line + `@consulting2.ai`

### Stories / Reels (1080×1920px vertical)
- Top 20%: logo + name + category badge
- Middle 60%: main content / demo
- Bottom 20%: CTA + handle
- Use `#0057FF` → `#040D20` subtle gradient bg
- Keep text in the center 80% (avoid edges)

### Carousel Posts
- Slide 1: Hook (Syne, large, white + mint accent)
- Slides 2–5: Content on `#071535` cards
- Last slide: CTA with icon mark + handle

## 📦 CANVA QUICK SETUP (Fastest path)

1. Go to **canva.com** → Brand Hub (free with account)
2. Upload logo: use your exported PNG
3. Add brand colors using HEX codes above
4. Add fonts: Syne (upload from Google Fonts) + Inter
5. Now every template auto-applies your brand

---

## 🎬 CAPCUT / PREMIERE LOWER THIRDS

For video lower thirds use this spec:
- Background pill: `#040D20` at 90% opacity, rounded 8px
- Left accent: 4px vertical bar in `#0057FF`
- Name text: Inter 500, white, 28pt
- Title text: JetBrains Mono, `#8A9BBE`, 18pt
- Logo watermark: top-right, 15% of frame width, 50% opacity

## ✅ QUICK CHECKLIST FOR EVERY ASSET

- [ ] Logo always has clear space (at least 1× icon height around it)
- [ ] Background is `#040D20` or `#071535` (never white unless forced)
- [ ] At least one `#00E599` accent element visible
- [ ] Headline in Syne (or Arial Black as fallback)
- [ ] Handle/URL always present on social assets
- [ ] No stretched or rotated logo
